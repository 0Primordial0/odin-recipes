Classic French Toast

Perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices thick bread (brioche or challah recommended)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup (120ml) milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)
  • Maple syrup and berries (to serve)

    • Steps

  • Whisk wet ingredients: In a shallow bowl, beat eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, sugar, and salt.
  • Soak bread: Dip each bread slice into the mixture for 15–20 seconds per side (don’t oversaturate).
  • Cook: Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Cook bread 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  • Serve: Top with maple syrup and fresh berries.
