Avocado & Egg Toast

A healthy and protein-packed breakfast with creamy avocado and a perfectly cooked egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread, toasted
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley (optional)

    • Steps

  • Mash avocado in a bowl and mix with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread the avocado mixture evenly on toasted bread.
  • Fry or poach eggs to your liking (runny yolk recommended).
  • Place eggs on top of the avocado toast.
  • Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and fresh herbs if desired.
  • Serve immediately.
