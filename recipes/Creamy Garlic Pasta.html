Creamy Garlic Pasta

A quick and flavorful pasta dish with a rich garlic cream sauce.

Ingredients

    (225g) fettuccine or spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Fresh

    Steps

  • Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  • Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  • Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  • Season with salt and black pepper.
  • Toss cooked pasta in the sauce until well coated.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
